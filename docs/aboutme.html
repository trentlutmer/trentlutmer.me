I’m Trent, a Korean American designer in Austin, TX. Currently working at IBM — concentrating on comprehensive product experiences and design systems.

My focus is on the humanity of what I'm designing and looking for its potential. I seek out those visceral feelings innate in us to help me identify those perspectives we don't understand.

Born in South Korea, adopted, and raised in Minnesota. I enjoy a good Neil Gaiman and Scott Snyder novel in my downtime, cooking to 80's power ballads, and adventures with my pug, Ruby.

P.S. This portfolio website is made using basic HTML and CSS. No javascript, frameworks, or templates. The code can be found on Github.
