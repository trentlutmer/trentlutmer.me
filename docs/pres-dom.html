Prescient Co. - UX Designer

Note: I have removed confidential information from this case study. Views reflected are mine alone.

The construction industry is slow to make changes, so it’s our job to balance the old and new to instill trust. Our system, Dom, allows us to set aside the visual aesthetics and focus on user problems.

To speed up our process, we met with the developers to implement an already made solution. We researched and collaborated with several teams and concluded that Material Design was best suited.
