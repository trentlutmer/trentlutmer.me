I’m Trent, an adopted Korean-America designer living in Denver, CO.

My focus is on the humanity of what I’m designing and look for its potential. I seek out those visceral feelings innate in all of us to help me identify those perspectives we don’t understand.

In my downtime, I enjoy a good Scott Snyder novel, cooking to 80’s power ballads, and adventures with my pug, Ruby.
