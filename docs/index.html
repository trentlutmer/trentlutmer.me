My name is Trent Lutmer and I enjoy bringing things to life on the web. I currently work at FiscalNote where I’m a product designer. On the side, I dabble in front-end development and illustration.

My focus on the humanity of what I’m designing and look to it’s potential. I seek out those visceral feelings innate in all of us to help me identify those perspectives we don’t understand.

I’m currently in Washington, DC with my pug Ruby. We both enjoy hiking long trails, reading good mystery books, and dreaming of living life on Mars.
