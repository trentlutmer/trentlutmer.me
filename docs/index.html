About me

My name is Trent Lutmer and I enjoy bringing things to life on the web. I currently work at FiscalNote where I’m a product designer. I am responsible for designing an Issues Management tool for political advocates who need to impact and influence legislators on polices. Working as a product designer, I discover, define, design, develop, and deliver solutions for FiscalNote’s web and mobile platforms. I spend my time collaborating with product managers, engineers, and other organization leads to evolve FiscalNote into a market-leading platform.

My focus is on the humanity of what I’m designing and look to it’s potential. I seek out those visceral feelings innate in all of us to help me identify those perspectives we don’t understand.
